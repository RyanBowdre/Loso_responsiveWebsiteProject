About Us

Loso is a desktop & mobile application built FOR gamers, BY gamers. Loso allows the user to search for, locate, and sign-up for gaming events anywhere within your city, with the touch of a button!

Why Choose Us?

Loso has a clean, easy-to-use, responsive interface that utilizes the latest technology to give YOU, the GAMER, the easiest experience in finding gaming events.

FAST RESPONSE TIME

Loso gives REAL-TIME updates on gaming scores, match results, upcoming competitors, and more in the blink of an eye. Loso will instantly give you the most up-to-date data from the event of your choice.

INSTANT ALERTS

Never miss another event. Loso takes in detailed preferences (game console, game genre, tournament location, number of entrants, etc) and immediately alerts you when a new event in your area is created.
